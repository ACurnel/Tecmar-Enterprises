ABOUT


Tecmar Enterprises was founded in 2002 to provide supply answers in multi-national markets. Clients soon required not only supply options and choices, but also comprehensive commercial strategies with pragmatic value creation. Through our group of highly qualified business and technical professionals, we build relationships with clients providing business, project, and supply chain development, including services for commercial operations, trade commerce, market analysis, and new company formation. With more than a half century of combined experience in multi-discipline, multi-national, and multi-cultural environments we meet and exceed our clients’ objectives for growth.


We excel with clients operating in constant-change markets which result from shifting geopolitical, regulatory, and industrial market trends. We adapt and embrace new opportunities and technologies to improve and ensure efficient, cost-effective delivery of products and services.

ABOUT


Tecmar Enterprises was founded in 2002 to provide supply answers in multi-national markets. Clients soon required not only supply options and choices, but also strategy and value creation. Subsequently, we build relationships providing services such as business development, supply sourcing, executive sales, and commercial operations including trade commerce, market analysis, and new company formation. More than a quarter century of experience in multi-national and multi-cultural environments supports this ability and opportunity.


We excel with clients operating in constant-change markets which result from shifting geopolitics, regulations, and market trends. We enjoy adapting to and embracing new trends such as E-commerce to improve and ensure efficient, cost-effective delivery of products and services.
